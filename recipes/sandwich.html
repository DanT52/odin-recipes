Sandwich

Description

Sandwiches are a very important type of food. It makes eating food more convineint. When you eat a sandwich you can eat it sometimes with just one hand, it makes it easier because it reqires less effort. Sandwiches were invented in the mid to late 1700s. Before then, people did not know what it means.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • meat
  • cheese
  • vegetables
  • Spread

Steps

  1. Take 2 slices of bread.
  2. Put spread on the bread if you wish.
  3. Put the ingredeints between the slices of bread.
  4. You now have a sandwich!
