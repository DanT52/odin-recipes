Bread

Description

Bread is a commonly eaten food and it is a key ingrediant in sandwiches. Without bread it is not possible to make a sandwich. For this reason it is very important that everyone knows how bread is created.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • salt
  • Yeast
  • Grains

Steps

  1. Mix The stuff together.
  2. Let the ingreadients rest.
  3. Kneading
  4. let it rise!
  5. Shape, then rise more!
  6. Time to bake the bread!
  7. Let the bread cool! Then eat it!
